Live Production Kanban

The workflow

From inquiry to dispatch.
Every step tracked.

1

Customer inquiry

Customer contacts you. Create a quotation in 30 seconds. Send via WhatsApp with a one-click approval link.

2

Quote approved

Customer taps Approve on their phone. Order is created automatically. You get an instant notification.

3

Production starts

Jobs appear on the Kanban board. Assign machines and staff. Track progress from design to press to finishing.

4

Invoice & dispatch

When all jobs are done, invoice is auto-generated. Mark as dispatched. Customer sees the update on their portal.

5

Business intelligence

AI analyses everything — revenue trends, customer risk, workload forecast, low stock alerts. Always one step ahead.
